Our Vision For Seamless Mobility

At the core of this groundbreaking initiative is a commitment to fill the void in Sri Lanka's transportation system—a lack of a fully functional Mobility as a Service (MaaS) platform. Our mission drives us to lead with a pioneering mobile-centric solution, seamlessly integrating real-time bus tracking and contactless fare payment. This convergence aims to redefine the accessibility and appeal of public transportation, propelling us towards a future marked by sustainability and technological innovation.

This journey is a dedication to ushering in a sustainable and technologically advanced future for public transportation. We envision a seamless experience for passengers, dissolving the complexities of commuting into the background. Join us as we navigate the corridors of change and embark on a trajectory towards a more connected, efficient, and advanced era in public transportation!"
ezBusPassenger:
Explore real-time bus tracking, plan your journey, and make contactless fare payments. Stay informed about bus arrivals, departures, and delays to ensure a convenient and stress-free travel experience.
ezBusConductor:
Effortlessly manage ticketing operations, monitor bus status in real time, and streamline the boarding process. Track ticket sales and ensure a smooth journey for passengers with this intuitive conductor-focused app.
ezBusManager:
Optimize your bus fleet with tools for scheduling, route planning, and performance monitoring. Analyze data for better decision-making and efficiently manage the logistics of your public transportation system. Empower bus managers to enhance overall fleet efficiency.

FAQ

How can I download the ezBus apps?

You can download the ezBus apps from the Google Play Store (for Android devices). Search for "ezBusPassenger," "ezBusConductor," or "ezBusManager" and follow the installation instructions.

Is ezBus available outside Colombo District?

Currently, ezBus is implemented for the Colombo District, covering both government and private buses. We have plans to expand our services to other districts in the near future.

Is my payment information secure with ezBus?

Yes, ezBus prioritizes the security of your payment information. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data and ensure a safe and reliable payment experience.

Can I use ezBus without an internet connection?

While some features may require an internet connection, the core functionalities of ezBus, such as real-time tracking and ticketing, generally work best with an active internet connection.
